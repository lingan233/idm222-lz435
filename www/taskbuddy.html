TaskBuddy Design

A product designed to reduce stress for commuters and general college students by task management and emotion monitoring.

Type

Design Thinking for Product Design class project


Date

Fall 2019


Role

User Research, Market Research, Ideations, Prototyping


Challenge

  • 35% of college students suffer from procrastination and mental disorders.
  • How may we help college commuters manage their work, feelings, and scheduleï¼Ÿ
  • User: college commuter and all students

Market Research

  • Researched...
    • College commuting

User Research

Breadth

Survey college commuters to find out the need for this product to exist by google survey. Posted a flyer in the commuter lounge that attracts participants by using an aspect of psycology. Found that there is a need for products that can improve commuter experience.

Survey Flyer

Depth

Interviewed commuters about their difficulty. Multiple participants expressed that they are baffled by commuting and do wish for something to help them. However, some of them do not have much hope to improve the situation for commuting.
